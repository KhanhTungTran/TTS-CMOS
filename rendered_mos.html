MOS Experiment

Evaluate the quality of speech systhesis

Thank you for participating in this experiment!

In this experiment, we use a computer to synthesize human speech. The following audio files contain real recordings and synthesized voices, and each sentence is accompanied by a transcript. The subjects were asked to listen with headphones and gave the following scores according to the quality of each sound file:

  • １（Very bad）
  • ２（Not good）
  • ３（Common）
  • ４（Good）
  • ５（Very good）
